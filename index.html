Hi 🤠

I'm Pranav, and I am a Software Engineer & Machine Learning Researcher.

I'm currently pursuing my Master's in Computer Science at The University of Texas at Arlington, on track to complete my B.S. and M.S. in five years.

This summer, I'll be joining Adobe in San Jose, CA as a Full-Time Software Engineer, working on Agentic AI within the Adobe Experience Platform. Along the way, I interned at companies across Dubai, Austin, and San Jose, including AMD and Adobe.

During my time in college, I co-authored two research papers published in top venues and earned over $120,000 in scholarships, including one that covered 80% of my bachelor's tuition. I also worked as a Machine Learning Research Assistant at the IDIR Lab @ UTA (best lab in the uni!), where I contributed a USDA-funded project GeneSieve. During this time, I was mentored by Dr. Chengkai Li, Director of the lab and Associate Chair of the CSE Department at UTA.

I am originally from India 🇮🇳, and grew up in Dubai, UAE 🇦🇪. Fun fact: My ancestral temple, built in 1112 CE, is a National Monument!
